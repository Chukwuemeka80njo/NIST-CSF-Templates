# CSF Risk Register ‚Äì Cybersecurity Risk Tracking & Analysis

The **CSF-Risk-Register** folder contains templates and tools for documenting, analyzing, and managing cybersecurity risks in alignment with the **NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)**. These documents support risk-informed decision-making and help maintain a consistent, auditable record of cybersecurity risks.

A risk register is a core component of any mature cybersecurity and GRC program.

## üìÇ Purpose of This Folder

This folder helps organizations:
- Maintain a centralized log of cybersecurity risks  
- Support CSF assessment findings and gap analysis  
- Track remediation and decision-making over time  
- Enable informed prioritization and planning  
- Demonstrate risk governance during audits and reviews  

---

## üìù How to Use

1. Start with the main Risk Register template to log each identified risk.  
2. Apply the scoring model to determine risk severity.  
3. Use heatmaps and ranking worksheets to prioritize items.  
4. Document treatment decisions (mitigate, accept, transfer).  
5. Update risk entries regularly and report to leadership.  

---

These templates align with **NIST CSF**, **NIST SP 800-30**, **NIST 800-53 RA controls**, **ISO 27005**, and general GRC risk management frameworks.
